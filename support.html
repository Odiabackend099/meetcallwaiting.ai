Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up call forwarding? +
Setting up call forwarding is easy! After you complete onboarding, you'll receive your AI phone number. Then simply dial *72 from your main phone, enter your AI number, and press #. We provide detailed instructions for all major carriers in your dashboard.
How long does setup take? +
Most businesses can complete the entire setup process in under 10 minutes. This includes creating your account, connecting your payment processor, linking your calendar, and setting up call forwarding.
When will I be charged? +
You'll be charged after your 14-day free trial ends. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged. All plans are billed monthly or annually, depending on your preference.
Can I change my plan anytime? +
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.
What if the AI doesn't understand a caller? +
Our AI is designed to handle various accents and speech patterns. If it can't understand a caller, it will politely ask them to repeat themselves or offer to transfer them to voicemail. You can review all interactions in your dashboard.
How do I customize the AI's responses? +
You can customize greetings, menu options, and responses from your dashboard. Professional and Enterprise plans offer advanced customization options including custom voice prompts and specialized workflows.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption for all data transmission and storage. We're SOC 2 compliant and follow strict data protection protocols. Your customer data is never shared with third parties.
Do I need to install any software? +
No software installation required! Callwaiting AI works entirely through your existing phone system. Just set up call forwarding and you're ready to go. You can manage everything through our web dashboard.
