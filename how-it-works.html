Call Forwarding Setup Guide

Follow these instructions to set up call forwarding with your carrier:

Verizon

  1. Dial *72 from your phone
  2. Enter your AI number when prompted
  3. Press # to confirm
  4. Wait for confirmation tone

AT&T

  1. Dial *72 from your phone
  2. Enter your AI number
  3. Press # to confirm
  4. Listen for confirmation

T-Mobile

  1. Dial *72 from your phone
  2. Enter your AI number
  3. Press # to confirm
  4. Wait for confirmation
