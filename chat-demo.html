How to Test the Chat Widget

  1. Look for the chat widget in the bottom-right corner of this page
  2. Click the chat icon to open the conversation window
  3. Try both text and voice modes using the toggle buttons
  4. For voice mode: Hold down the voice button to speak
  5. Ask questions about CallWaiting.ai, business communication, or anything else
  6. Listen to the AI responses with high-quality neural voices
  7. Test on mobile by resizing your browser window
